Preserving Traditional Martial Arts

The Zen Bei Butoku Kai is dedicated to the preservation and teaching of traditional martial arts as passed down by O'Sensei Richard Kim. His teachings encompass not just the physical techniques of Karate, Kobudo, and Tai Chi, but the philosophical and spiritual depth of the martial way.

"To win one hundred victories in one hundred battles is not the highest skill. To subdue the enemy without fighting is the highest skill."
- The Classical Man
